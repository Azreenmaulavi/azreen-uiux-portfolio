Case Study : ApniSabzi — Digitizing Local Street Vegetable Vendors

ApniSabzi is a UI/UX case study focused on helping local street vendors connect with nearby customers through a clean and accessible digital marketplace experience.

The project focused on solving problems like low visibility, difficult vendor discovery, and lack of trust in local grocery shopping while maintaining a simple experience for everyday users.

  • Conducted user research & competitor analysis
  • Designed responsive web experience & user flows
  • Created wireframes, UI system & interactive prototype
